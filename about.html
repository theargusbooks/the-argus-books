ABOUT US

Who We Are

THE ARGUS BOOKS is committed to publishing works that illuminate culture, celebrate heritage, and inspire deeper understanding. We specialize in creating premium-quality books — from fiction to non-fiction, and from scholarly works to timeless coffee table editions.

With a strong focus on design, editorial precision, and cultural authenticity, we aim to curate books that stay with the reader long after the final page. Every title we produce is shaped with care, craftsmanship, and intention.

◆

Our Mission

Our mission is to publish works that preserve culture, spark curiosity, and encourage meaningful conversations. We strive to bring out voices that reflect depth, beauty, and intellectual richness.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create timeless books that celebrate art, heritage, and human expression — crafted with care, authenticity, and purpose.

◆

What Makes Us Different

  • Premium-quality production with meticulous attention to detail.
  • Deep respect for cultural heritage and artistic traditions.
  • A modern design philosophy blended with timeless aesthetics.
  • Strong editorial craftsmanship and research-backed content.
  • Commitment to authors, artists, and cultural practitioners.
◆

Our Journey

From our early beginnings to the publication of acclaimed coffee table books, THE ARGUS BOOKS continues to grow as a house that values substance, beauty, and meaningful storytelling.

Our journey is shaped by our authors, our readers, and our commitment to publishing works that leave a lasting impact.

◆

Founder’s Message

At THE ARGUS BOOKS, our journey began with a simple belief — that books carry the power to preserve culture, ignite curiosity, and shape the way we understand the world. Every title we publish is crafted with care, purpose, and a deep respect for heritage.

Our commitment is to bring meaningful stories forward, elevate voices that matter, and create books that stand the test of time. Thank you for being part of our journey.

— Ashwani Sharma
Founder, THE ARGUS BOOKS
