ABOUT US

Who are We

The Argus Books is an independent publishing house based in India, committed to producing high-quality books across genres including fiction, non-fiction, culture, art, self-help, history, philosophy, and regional literature. Our mission is to bring meaningful, inspiring, and intellectually enriching content to readers in India and across the world.

We collaborate with emerging as well as established authors, giving them a platform to reach a wide audience. Our catalogue focuses on thoughtful storytelling, well-researched non-fiction, and visually rich coffee-table books. We aim to preserve cultural heritage and promote new voices by publishing books that carry depth, emotion, and artistic value.

Our goal is to empower writers, support creativity, and contribute to the growth of Indian publishing by maintaining professionalism, transparency, and editorial excellence.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Submit your synopsis in clean, double-spaced text.
  • Avoid fancy fonts; use simple, readable formatting.
  • If submitting in a language other than English, send it in PDF format.
  • Include complete contact details with your submission.
  • Send at least 3‚Äì4 sample chapters for evaluation.
  • Do not send original manuscripts unless requested.
  • If your work is approved, we will request the complete manuscript.
